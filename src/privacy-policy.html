Privacy Policy

Your privacy really matters

We have a few fundamental principles:
- We don’t ask you for personal information unless we truly need it.
- Here we only ask you about your age range and nationality because there are factors that could help see trends in perception of the sustainability lexicon. We do not collect other personal information.
- We don’t share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law, or protect our rights.
- We don’t sell any of your personal information.


Questions and concerns

If you want to exercise the applicable rights (such as access, modification, ...)  or if you have any questions or comments, you are more than welcome to contact us at contact@regenerative-voices.fr


Information we collect

We only collect information about you if we have a reason to do so. We collect information in two ways:
- Information You Provide to Us : We collect information if and when you provide information to us via the survey, but there is no personal information collected that could identify you.
- Information automatically collected: We and our partners use cookies. Users can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. For more information about our use of cookies and other tracking technologies, as well as how to opt out of the use of cookies, please refer to our Cookie Policy here.


How we use information

We use information about you as mentioned above and as follows:
- To monitor and protect the security of our Services, detect and prevent illegal activities, fight spam, and protect the rights and property of frog and EDF and others.


Compliance

We do not hold any personal information about you nor contact.


Choices

You have several choices available when it comes to information about you:
- Limit the Information that You Provide
- Set Your Browser to Reject Cookies: visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using our websites, with the drawback that certain features may not function properly without the aid of cookies.


Privacy Police Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, we may change its Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.


Contacting us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise any of your rights as described above, please contact us at contact@regenerative-voices.fr. We will attempt to resolve any complaints regarding the use of your Personal Information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

If after contacting us, you are still not satisfied with our response, you can contact your regulatory authority: https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/bo
