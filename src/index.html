Let's build a regenerative future together

frogLab and EDF Pulse Design wish to explore what is beyond Net Zero. Reducing our carbon footprint is necessary, but it is not enough. We believe aiming for radically positive and desirable outcomes for all forms of life can create a shift of mindset supporting collective action to do more good instead of doing less bad.

The first step according to us is to imagine this future and voice it. Take part in a short experience to voice your opinion and contribute to a collective map of the words people relate to most.

COPNAMES

The collective word map

The collective word map combines the answers of every person who's taken the survey before you. They show that each of us start from different positions and values. By gaining awareness about them, we can set achievable goals and start our journey. Our combined paths can complement each other, together we can create a regenerative world.
